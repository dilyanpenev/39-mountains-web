About

The App

39 Mountains is an iOS app for hikers who want to track their progress conquering Bulgaria's iconic mountain summits. Log your ascents, earn achievements, explore an interactive map, and share your stats with friends.

FAQ

How do I log a summit?

Open the mountain's detail page and tap "Mark as Summited." Your achievement will be saved to your profile and counted toward your progress.

How many mountains are there in Bulgaria?

By official records Bulgaria has 39 mountains.

Is my data private?

Your summit log and profile information are private by default. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
